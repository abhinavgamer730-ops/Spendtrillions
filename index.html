❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do you play the Spend 1 Trillion Dollars Game?
The goal is simple: start with a balance of $1 Trillion (or choose from Level 1-4 budgets) and spend it all until you reach zero. Buy items from various categories like everyday goods, luxury, property, transport, and mega items. You can also risk your cash at the reverse-casino terminal where losing the bet burns your money (bringing you closer to victory) and winning doubles your cash.
Can you spend Elon Musk's money in this simulator?
Yes! The game features a dedicated 'Elon Musk Mode' (Level 4) with a massive $5 Trillion budget, allowing players to experience the scale of the world's first trillionaire's wealth by trying to spend it all on mega assets.
Can you actually spend a trillion dollars in this simulator?
Yes, but it takes strategic planning! Low-tier items cost very little, while high-tier mega items like skyscrapers, space stations, and buying out companies consume billions. Use the multi-buy features, invest in fluctuating markets, and leverage the Decryptor Casino to accelerate your spending.
Who created the Spend 1 Trillion Dollars Game?
The game was designed and created by Abhinav Gupta, a developer focusing on clean web experiences, with professional assistance from his brother Ansh Vaish for system balancing and core mechanics optimization.
What other web tools has the developer built?
In addition to this game, the developer has built a live mutual fund SIP Calculator (Systematic Investment Plan) and Space Shooter X, showing a range of mathematical simulators and HTML5 game projects.
